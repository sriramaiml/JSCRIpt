Article 1

Turmeric is a versatile spice that is widely used in various cuisines around the world. It has got a warm, earthy flavor along with a vibrant golden-yellow color, which makes it an attractive ingredient in savory dishes such as curries, stews, and soups. It is frequently used to flavor and naturally color rice dishes, vegetables, curry powders, mustards, butter, cheese, and meats. Turmeric is also used in desserts like puddings, and custards, and drinks like golden milk.
